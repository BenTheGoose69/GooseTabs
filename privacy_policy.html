Privacy Policy

GooseTabs: The Tab Writer

Introduction

Welcome to GooseTabs: The Tab Writer ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how our application handles your information.

This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and disclosure when you use our mobile application GooseTabs: The Tab Writer (the "App").

Key Point: GooseTabs is designed with privacy in mind. The App operates entirely offline and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Information Collection and Use

Data We Do NOT Collect

GooseTabs does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Crash reports
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any data transmitted over the internet

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

GooseTabs stores the following information exclusively on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us:

Data Type Purpose Storage Location
Guitar/Bass Tabs To save your created tablature compositions Device only
Folders To organize your tabs into categories Device only
App Preferences To remember your theme, color scheme, and other settings Device only

Note: All data is stored using your device's local storage (SharedPreferences). We have no ability to access, view, or retrieve this information.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Since GooseTabs does not collect any user data, we do not share any personal information with third parties. Specifically:

  • We do not sell your data to anyone
  • We do not share data with advertisers
  • We do not share data with analytics providers
  • We do not share data with any third-party services

Export Functionality

The App allows you to export your tabs as text files. When you use this feature, you are in full control of the exported data and where it is shared. The export functionality uses your device's native sharing capabilities and we have no access to the files you choose to share.

Third-Party Services

GooseTabs does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media platforms. The App functions entirely offline and independently.

Data Security

Your data security is important to us. Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features
  • No data is transmitted over the internet, eliminating network-based vulnerabilities
  • Only you have access to your created tabs and settings

We recommend keeping your device's operating system updated and using device-level security features (such as screen lock) to protect your locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

GooseTabs does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since our App does not collect any data, it is safe for users of all ages.

Parents and guardians can be assured that their children's privacy is protected when using GooseTabs, as no personal data is collected or transmitted.

Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored on your device and accessible only to you
  • Deletion: You can delete individual tabs, folders, or clear all app data through your device's app settings
  • Export: You can export your tabs at any time using the built-in export feature
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data

Permissions

GooseTabs may request the following permissions:

Permission Purpose Required
Storage (if applicable) To export tab files to your device's Downloads folder Optional

The App only uses requested permissions for their stated purposes. You can manage permissions through your device's settings at any time.
